# Scaling Roadmap Checklist

## Stage 0: IMPROVISE
- [ ] Identified a problem people have
- [ ] Created a solution that works
- [ ] Got first paying customer
- [ ] Can deliver result consistently

## Stage 1: MONETIZE
- [ ] 10+ paying customers
- [ ] Know what they pay for
- [ ] Price is profitable
- [ ] Can acquire customers manually

## Stage 2: ADVERTISE
- [ ] Running paid ads profitably
- [ ] CAC < LTV
- [ ] Have winning creative
- [ ] Can scale ad spend

## Stage 3: STABILIZE
- [ ] Systems documented
- [ ] Team hired for delivery
- [ ] Can take 2-week vacation
- [ ] Operations don't depend on founder

## Stage 4: PRIORITIZE
- [ ] Identified highest leverage activity
- [ ] Cut low-value activities
- [ ] Focused on 1-2 growth channels
- [ ] Saying no to distractions

## Stage 5: PRODUCTIZE
- [ ] Delivery is standardized
- [ ] Can onboard new customers without calls
- [ ] Margins improving
- [ ] Scalable infrastructure

## Stage 6: OPTIMIZE
- [ ] Tracking all key metrics
- [ ] A/B testing regularly
- [ ] Margins at industry high
- [ ] Churn under control

## Stage 7: CATEGORIZE
- [ ] Own a category
- [ ] Known for specific thing
- [ ] Media mentions
- [ ] Industry recognition

## Stage 8: SPECIALIZE
- [ ] Best in world at one thing
- [ ] Premium pricing justified
- [ ] Competition irrelevant
- [ ] Customers seek you out

## Stage 9: CAPITALIZE
- [ ] Exit options evaluated
- [ ] Valuation maximized
- [ ] Clean financials
- [ ] Decision made: exit or hold
