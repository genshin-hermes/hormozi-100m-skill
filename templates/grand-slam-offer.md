## Value Equation Check

- [ ] Dream outcome is specific and measurable
- [ ] Perceived likelihood is high (proof, guarantee)
- [ ] Time delay is minimized
- [ ] Effort & sacrifice are minimized
- [ ] Price is 10-25% of total value
- [ ] Guarantee reverses risk
- [ ] Scarcity is real, not fake
- [ ] Bonuses are relevant and valuable

## Test Plan

1. Pitch to 10 prospects
2. Record objections

4. Test again with 10 more
